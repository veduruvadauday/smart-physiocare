Cancer Rehabilitation



Cancer


A set of disorders where uncontrolled cell division and growth occur is also commonly referred to as "cancer." Cancer can generate a variety of symptoms, depending on its form and type.
Physiotherapy can lessen the physical effects of cancer while increasing a person's capacity for an active, fulfilling life.


Types of cancer :

Cancer can be either benign or malignant. A malignant tumor can spread and infiltrate other organs through the lymphatic system or bloodstream. Benign tumors are incapable of progressing in the same way.




The genesis of a tumor affects the additional classification of the tumor. The term "carcinoma" refers to the most prevalent types of cancer that appear in the breast, prostate, pancreatic, or lung. Sarcoma is a type of cancer that develops in bone or cartilage. Cancer that develops in the blood cells is known as lymphoma or leukemia.

Symptoms of Cancer :

  • • Pain
  • • Lumps
  • • Swelling
  • • Bleeding
  • • Unexplained weight loss
  • • Fatigue
  • • Excessive sweating

The above symptoms may occur in individuals who do not have cancer; therefore they do not confirm the diagnosis. It is diagnosed based on the medical signs and symptoms, blood tests, computed tomography (CT) scan and a microscopic examination of a biopsy sample. This helps the medical staff to understand the nature and the extent of progression.

Physiotherapy for Cancer:
Physiotherapy can help with symptoms and issues associated with the type and location of the tumour, whether a person chooses to accept or reject cancer treatment. An expert physiotherapist will conduct a complete assessment of the patient's symptoms and concerns, talk about attainable goals in a given time frame, and develop a treatment strategy tailored to the individual's needs.

The SMART Physio Care Cancer Rehabilitation can be divided into 4 categories:

  • • Before Surgery: Before their cancer surgery, patients are intended to benefit from it. To determine any issues that might hinder the recovery following surgery and return to work, a pre-operative assessment and education. Each patient will have a personalized pre-operative rehabilitation regimen that consists of education, operational advice, and targeted exercises to build muscle and increase stamina.
  • • After Surgery: It was created to aid in the healing process of patients who had recently undergone surgery. It consists of manual physiotherapy and modified exercises to maximize recovery from surgery, as well as education and functional guidance for home and work. It aims to restore strength and flexibility to specific joints and muscle groups that have become weak and inflexible due to surgery.
  • • During Cancer Treatments: During cancer treatments, it is intended to boost energy levels and maintain health to lessen the toll on physical performance and general fitness. In order to reduce time away from work, muscle atrophy, and functional impairment, it tries to assist patients in managing tiredness, flexibility, strength, and stamina. It includes workouts to increase abdominal strength and spinal stability, information about fatigue management, home and work-based adaptations, and cardiovascular activity to focus on stretching and strengthening exercises for the upper and lower extremities.
  • • Overall Fitness: It is intended to help with overall fitness and boost physical endurance after treatments are over. It seeks to improve cardiovascular fitness, strength, and flexibility to increase endurance, metabolism, muscular tone, and body composition. The exercise selection focuses on enhancing physical functioning to maximize healing for a return to work and leading a productive life. Fatigue management education, return to work, sports, and leisure activities.

Physiotherapy can significantly shorten a cancer patient's healing period. It can help you move around more effectively and increase your ability to complete daily duties. Individuals with cancer can receive physiotherapy treatments at Smart Physio Care, at home, in the gym, or in the hydrotherapy pool.
