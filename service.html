Frequently Asked
Questions.

  • What services does Smart Physio Care Hospital provide?
    Smart Physio Care Hospital offers a wide range of physiotherapy services, including assessment and diagnosis of musculoskeletal conditions, personalized treatment plans, rehabilitation exercises, manual therapy techniques, electrotherapy modalities, pain management, post-operative care, and more.
  • How can I schedule an appointment?
    You can schedule an appointment at Smart Physio Care Hospital by calling our reception desk during working hours. Our staff will assist you in finding a convenient date and time for your appointment.
  • What should I wear for my physiotherapy session?
    It's recommended to wear loose, comfortable clothing that allows easy movement during your physiotherapy session. Depending on your condition, you may be asked to change into a gown or specific attire to facilitate assessment and treatment.
